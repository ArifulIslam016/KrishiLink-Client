
# KrishiLink
Live Link: https://krishi-link-3accb.web.app


**Project Overview:**  
KrishiLink is a modern full-stack, agriculture-focused platform built to connect our respected farmers. It helps to managing ingidents of farming and keep our farmers safe from syndicate. Also they have an oppetunity to join a community thow the app
